Stones

Granite

Granite is a durable, coarse-grained igneous rock made up of quartz, feldspar, and mica minerals. It is widely used in construction and countertops due to its strength and attractive appearance, which can vary from pink and gray to black and speckled.

Diorite

Diorite is a tough, coarse-grained igneous rock composed of feldspar, hornblende, and/or biotite. It's commonly used in construction due to its durability and is often gray or black.

Porphyry

Porphyry is an igneous rock with large crystals in a fine-grained matrix, known for its speckled appearance. It has been used in architecture and art for its durability and aesthetic appeal. Its prominent crystals are typically composed of minerals like feldspar or quartz.
